Policies



Financing

You and you pet deserve the best. Thats why Durham House of Pets offers zero interest financing* on purchases of $500 or more. Just fill out our Financing application form and pass a 'soft'** credit check to receive financing with down payments as low as $59.99.

*For first 12 months, thereafter subject to 12% APR.
**Credit check may affect your credit score.


Delivery

Don't have enough room in the trunk for your new cat-castle or fish tank? DHP offers free home delivery* on all purchases and installation for all large tanks and aquariums. Our certified delivery team will make sure your purchases get home safe and sound, and we'll even stay to get your aquarium set up and running before we leave.

*Delivery window between 14 and 35 business days after purchase.


Return Policies

Returns accepted for unopened pet food and other packaged goods. All sales on apparel, tanks, toys, beds, fish, and other supplies are final.
