About Us



History

I started the Durham House of Pets back in 1998, after multiple interactions with big name pet stores left a bad, impersonal taste in my mouth. We began as a small pet food retailer with a focus on nutritious and organic kibble. Today, DHP has grown to a multi-region, one-stop shop for all your pet supply needs, while maintaining the homegrown, local business feel that we set out to create. -Kholekile Gwebu, Founder of DHP




Why Use Us

At Durham House of Pets, we treat every dog, cat, and fish as if it were our own. That means we do not sell any pet food, toys, tanks, or apparel that we would'nt use ourselves. You can rest assured that you and your furry friend will leave completely satisfied with our selection, products, and service. It is each and every DHP employee's goal to make your pet supply shopping experience fun and to make you feel like a welcome member of the DHP family.
